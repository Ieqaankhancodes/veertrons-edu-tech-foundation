## 2. "What We Do" Section (Core Services)
*We can divide the 9 services you mentioned into 3 clean, professional categories on the website:*

### Category A: Education, Training & Career 
*(Focusing on students and graduates)*
*   **Skill Training & Placement:** Comprehensive training programs with dedicated placement activities to help students secure their future.
*   **Career Guidance (10th & 12th):** Helping young minds make the right educational choices at the most crucial turning points of their lives.
*   **Professional Internships:** Providing real-world industry exposure with internships tailored for BCA, MCA, BE, and Non-VTU students.
*   **Bi-Annual Job Fairs:** Hosting major job fairs every 6 months to connect talented youth directly with top employers in the industry.

### Category B: Social Impact & Community Well-being
*(Focusing on the broader society)*
*   **Women Empowerment:** Dedicated initiatives to skill, support, and uplift women, fostering independence and leadership.
*   **Health Initiatives:** Driving health awareness, medical camps, and wellness programs for the underprivileged.
*   **Community Development:** Building stronger, self-sustaining communities through localized social programs.

### Category C: Early Childhood Care
*(Focusing on the youngest minds)*
*   **Play School & Kindergarten:** A nurturing, safe, and engaging environment providing high-quality foundational education for young children.
